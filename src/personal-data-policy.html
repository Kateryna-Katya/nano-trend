Personal Data Processing Policy

1.2. This Policy applies to all information that the Operator may receive about visitors (hereinafter — "Users") of the nano-trend.org website.

2. Key Definitions Used in the Policy

  • Website — a collection of graphic and information materials, as well as computer programs and databases, ensuring their availability on the Internet at nano-trend.org.

  • Personal Data — any information relating directly or indirectly to a specific User of the website.
